«Better Expert Rarity» is a simple mod that changes «Expert Mode» and «Master Mode» rarities (Ye, mod name says that only «Expert Mode» rarity has been changed, but it's not)

[hr][/hr]

Changes:
• The colors of rarities have been changed to gradient
• All «Expert/Master Mode» items now have rarity of -12/-13
• After reforging, items remain with expert/master mode rarity
