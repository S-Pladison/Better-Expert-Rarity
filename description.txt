[i]«Better Expert Rarity»[/i] is a simple mod that changes «Expert Mode» and «Master Mode» rarities (Ye, mod name says that only the expert has been changed, but this is not so)
[hr][/hr]
[b]Changes:[/b]
[list]
[*]The colors of rarities have been changed to gradient (At the moment, only when hovering over item)
[*]All «Expert/Master Mode» items now have rarity of -12/-13
[*]After reforging, items remain with expert/master mod rarity
[/list]
